Live data from a Precision Infrared Radiometer (Pyrgeometer, PIR). The sensor is up on the meteorological sensor mast.

The Eppley PIR contains two thermistors in addition to the thermopile. Temperature readings from the thermistors can be used to compensate for error introduced by the case and dome termperature.

This sensor is polled every 30 seconds. Data are live from the sensors (not retrieved from log/database).

Individual time series can be toggled ON/OFF by clicking the corresponding legend. This page turns grayscale if the data is stale.
