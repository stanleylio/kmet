Live data from a Bosch BME280 Temperature, Barometric Pressure and Relative Humidity sensor housed inside the electrical box up on the meteorological sensor mast. This sensor is polled once a minute. Data are live from the sensors (not retrieved from log/database).

Being inside the electrical box, the sensor is measuring the temperature and humidity inside the box. Pressure measurement, on the other hand, should be the same as barometric pressure since the electrical box is not hermetically sealed.

Individual time series can be toggled ON/OFF by clicking the corresponding legend. This page turns grayscale if the data is stale.
